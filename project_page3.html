About this area

San Diego

Located by the sea, Pendry San Diego is in San Diego's Gaslamp Quarter neighborhood, an area with good airport proximity and shopping. Seaport Village and Westfield Horton Plaza are worth checking out if shopping is on the agenda, while those wishing to experience the area's natural beauty can explore Balboa Park and Waterfront Park. Looking to enjoy an event or a game? See what's going on at Petco Park or SDCCU Stadium. Our guests appreciate the hotel's location for the sightseeing.

About this property

Pendry San Diego

This smoke-free hotel features 3 restaurants, a full-service spa, and an outdoor pool. WiFi in public areas is free. Other amenities include 3 bars/lounges, a 24-hour fitness center, and a poolside bar. All 317 rooms offer comforts like pillowtop beds and Frette Italian sheets, plus free WiFi and flat-screen TVs with cable channels. Guests will also find 24-hour room service, minibars, and rainfall showerheads.
